About

I’m the founder of CoachSense, but before that I’m a father, entrepreneur and someone who believes our greatest potential begins with understanding ourselves.

For more than 20 years I’ve built a technology company and led a team through the challenges of growth. Alongside that, health, endurance sport and personal development have taught me the same lesson: lasting progress depends on resilience, recovery, reflection and purpose.

CoachSense was created from that belief.

Life can feel noisy. There is more information, comparison and digital distraction than ever, but more input does not always bring greater clarity. I wanted to create technology that helps you pause, notice what matters and better understand yourself.

CoachSense is my answer.

Rather than telling you what to do, CoachSense supports small daily actions across four foundations: Resilience, Recovery, Reflection and Purpose. It helps you build emotional awareness, confidence and the ability to make thoughtful decisions in a complex world.

As AI becomes part of everyday life, these human qualities matter even more. Technology should strengthen your judgement, not replace it. My hope is that CoachSense becomes a trusted companion as you build your inner sense, one small step at a time.

Julian Matthews
